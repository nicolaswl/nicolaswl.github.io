About Me

I'm a passionate and relentless tinkerer seeking a software engineering internship for the 2021 summer to direct my web development, design research, and project team experience toward an impact in at least one user’s life

I’m in my third year at UC San Diego as a Computer Science major. Here, I'm a part of Triton Software Engineering, making web apps to help nonprofit organizations. In this community, I regularly work in project teams and interact with both stakeholders and product users. My team lately finished our contribution to the Key Conservation app that provides an easy social media presence for nonprofits conservation organizations to connect with volunteers.

I’ve also worked as a research assistant in the Design Lab, where I’ve helped cultivate new ways to frame design tasks that encourage problem-solvers to explore a wide range of ideas. I’ve learned that the best ideas come from effective listening that captures the full context of a user and their problem.

There are numerous product teams that I am proud to have been a part of through my coursework and time at UCSD and I encourage you to take a look!

I want to combine my extensive full stack development experience with my relentless curiosity and human-centered design focus to make solutions that matter. I look forward to getting to know the needs of your users and becoming a member of your team!
